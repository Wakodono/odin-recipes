Lasagne

Description

Spinach lasagne is a delicious and wholesome Italian dish that features layers of lasagne noodles, ricotta or cottage cheese, marinara sauce, and, of course, spinach. The spinach adds a nutritious and flavorful element to the dish, complementing the cheesy layers and savory tomato sauce. This vegetarian option provides a satisfying and comforting meal, showcasing the harmonious combination of pasta, cheese, and vibrant spinach flavors.

Ingredients

  • Lasagne noodles (about 9-12 sheets)
  • 2 cups fresh spinach, chopped
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese or cottage cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 cups marinara sauce (homemade or store-bought)
  • 4 cups marinara sauce (homemade or store-bought)
  • Olive oil for greasing the baking dish
  • Optional: 1/2 cup chopped onions and 2 cloves minced garlic for extra flavor

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven:

    Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. Cook Lasagne Noodles:

    Cook the lasagne noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.

  3. Prepare Filling:

    In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta or cottage cheese, chopped spinach, egg, grated Parmesan, oregano, basil, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Mix well.

  4. Saute Onions and Garlic (Optional):

    If using, sauté chopped onions and minced garlic in olive oil until softened.

  5. Layering:
    • Grease a baking dish with olive oil.
    • Place a layer of cooked lasagne noodles at the bottom of the dish.
    • Spread a portion of the cheese and spinach filling over the noodles.
    • Optionally, add a layer of sautéed onions and garlic.
    • Pour a layer of marinara sauce over the filling.
    • Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese.
  6. Repeat Layers:

    Repeat the layering process until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of marinara sauce and shredded mozzarella on top.

  7. Bake:
    • Cover the baking dish with foil.
    • Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes or until the lasagne is hot and bubbly.
  8. Broil (Optional):

    If you like a golden-brown top, uncover and broil for an additional 2-3 minutes.

  9. Rest and Serve:
    • Allow the lasagne to rest for a few minutes before slicing.
    • Serve warm and enjoy your delicious spinach lasagne!
